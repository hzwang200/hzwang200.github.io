Introduction

Currently I work with Prof. Weiwei Xie on quantum materials.

I obtained my B.S. degree in Chemistry (Poling Class) at Nankai University in June 2021. During my stay there, I have been worked with Prof. Bin Zhao at Nankai on MOF-based heterogeneous catalysis, Prof. Sara Thoi at Johns Hopkins on electrochemical CO2 reduction, Prof. Qilong Shen at SIOC on trifluoromethoxyl metal complexes, and Prof. Qianrong Fang at Jilin U on COFs crystallinity to explore my interests at the corner of inorganic chemistry and materials science.
