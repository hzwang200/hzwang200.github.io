Meet Haozhe

I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. degree in Chemistry at Rutgers University. Here I work with Prof. Weiwei Xie on the discovery and development of novel superconductive materials applying solid-state synthesis in ambient and high pressure conditions.
I obtained my B.S. degree in Chemistry (Poling Class) at Nankai University. During my undergraduate, I worked in several different labs (with Prof. Bin Zhao at Nankai, Prof. Sara Thoi at Johns Hopkins, Prof. Qilong Shen at SIOC, and Prof. Qianrong Fang) to explore my interests at the corner of inorganic chemistry and materials science, in particular, on metal complexes and porous framework materials.
