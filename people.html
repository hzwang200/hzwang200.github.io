About Me

I am currently pursuing my bachelor's degree (B.S. in Chemistry, expected June 2021) at Nankai University. At Nankai, I worked with Professor Bin Zhao on the design, synthesis, and characterization of novel metal-organic frameworks (MOFs) and their applications in heterogeneous catalysis.
xxx
In summer 2019, I went to work with Professor V. Sara Thoi in the Department of Chemistry at Johns Hopkins University as a visiting undergraduate researcher. There I got familiar with the development of competent molecular catalysts for electrochemical carbon dioxide reduction. Particularly, we investigated the redox properties of one novel rhenium tricarbonyl complex and its catalytic responses to targeted reactions.
